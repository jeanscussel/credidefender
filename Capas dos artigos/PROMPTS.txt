MODULO 3
____________________________________________________________

An ultra-realistic image of a mysterious, hooded, hacker-style figure.
He is sitting by a small lake in the middle of a dark, dense, closed forest, where it is impossible to see through the trees because there is only darkness.
The small lake is a giant square screen lying on the ground as if it were part of that environment, attached to the ground and perfectly connected to it. the screen is face up, lying down on the floor. The border of screen is not a light border, it is a real screen bord.
The man is sitting next to the screen on the ground in a fishing chair with a simple fishing rod in his hand, fishing inside this monitor on the ground.
Inside the monitor are people in the form of light that resemble lightning people, among them one is trying to catch the bait, which is a small, shiny email icon.
The place inside the screen where the people are swimming appears to be shimmering and magical.
The whole environment is nocturnal under the moonlight, which is only partially visible through small spaces in the treetops; everything has a very dark and tense air of suspense, extreme darkness and horror.

MODULO 5
____________________________________________________________
Uma imagem ultra realista. 
Um saguão gigante e branco, paredes brancas, chão branco e teto branco. 
Várias pessoas estão segurando peças de quebra-cabeça gigantes de aproximadamente 2 metros cada.  
Algumas peças já estão montadas e outras estão sendo levadas, algumas por uma pessoa, outras em duplas. 
As peças montadas estão revelando uma parte de uma palavra que é "CONF". 
Todas as peças são brancas e pretas, nas peças qeu estão montadas a parte preta é a que forma a parte da palavra já aparente. 
Algumas pessoas estão penduradas pois a estrutura é alta(cerca de 3 peças de altura). 
A ideia é que essas pessoas estão trabalhando em conjunto para formar a palavra "CONFIANÇA" em idioma português do Brasil. 
NO chão em um dos cantos é possível ver o gabarito com o resultado.

____________________________________________________________
An ultra-hyper-realistic image of a person walking across a cliff on top of a giant network cable.
The walls on either side of the cliff are, on one side, the back of a gigantic computer case attached to the cliff; it's possible to identify the end of the cable and know that it's a network cable.
On the other side, there is no cliff, but rather clouds representing the internet, where the cable disappears into them in the distance.
The cable is approximately 15cm in diameter.
The person walking on the cable is a woman, looking at her cell phone and walking towards the clouds, which can be seen far ahead; the computer is behind her.
This person is wearing a yellow dress in a 60s-70s rock style.
It is essential that the person is walking towards the clouds with their back to the computer, and that the cable is visible disappearing into the clouds.
